Description

LumiSkin is an AI-powered skin analysis web application that transforms a simple face image into a personalized skincare intelligence report. By combining computer vision, machine learning, and user-provided inputs, LumiSkin detects key skin concerns such as pigmentation, acne, wrinkles, pores, texture, and skin age within seconds.

Unlike Traditional skincare consultations that require time and cost, LumiSkin provides an instant, data-driven skin assessment through an intuitive online experience.

Users upload a photo, the AI analyzes multiple skin parameters, and a preview of the results is shown. To unlock the complete diagnostic report, users can pay a small fee or subscribe for unlimited access.

The platform goes beyond analysis by offering a personalized morning and night skincare routine, ingredient guidance, and smart product suggestions tailored to the user’s skin condition and budget.

LumiSkin’s mission is to democratize skincare by making advanced skin analysis accessible to everyone, empowering users with knowledge and personalized solutions for healthier, glowing skin.

With a user-friendly interface, fast processing, and clinically validated AI models, LumiSkin is revolutionizing how people understand and care for their skin in the digital age.
