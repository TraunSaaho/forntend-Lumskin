## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Core Terms
- **Authentication**: Verifying who you are (login/signup)
- **Authorization**: Determining what you can access
- **Session**: Your active login state
- **Token**: Secure identifier for your session
- **Firebase**: Backend service for auth and data
- **Firestore**: Cloud database for user data

### Important Features
- ✅ **Email Verification**: Required step after signup
- ✅ **Remember Me**: Saves your email for convenience
- ✅ **Password Reset**: Recover forgotten passwords
- ✅ **Form Validation**: Catches errors before submission
- ✅ **Session Management**: Keeps you logged in securely
- ✅ **OAuth**: Google login option

### Security Concepts
- 🔐 **Encryption**: Securing data in transit and at rest
- 🔐 **Hashing**: One-way encryption for passwords
- 🔐 **Tokens**: Secure identifiers for authenticated users
- 🔐 **Validation**: Checking data before processing
- 🔐 **Rate Limiting**: Preventing brute force attacks
- 🔐 **Session Isolation**: Keeping your data private
